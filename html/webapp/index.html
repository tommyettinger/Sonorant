Twirly Swirly Rainbow Glowing Noise Viewer

Controls:

  • 'f' decreases the frequency or speed of the effect (with Shift, it increases frequency). This can be held down.
  • 'v' decreases the variation between hues used (with Shift, it increases variation). This can be held down.
  • 'a' decreases the 'a' parameter to the lightness change (with Shift, it increases 'a'). This can be held down.
  • 'b' decreases the 'b' parameter to the lightness change (with Shift, it increases 'b'). This can be held down.
  • 's' moves the seed down by 0.25, or up by 0.25 if shift is held.
  • '/' (slash) randomizes the seed.
  • 'c' changes the shader from ridged noise to FBM noise, or back again.
  • 'r' restarts the animation to its initial form (at time 0).
  • 'p' prints the current frames per second (higher means better performance) to the log.
  • On desktop only, Alt-Enter will toggle fullscreen mode.
  • On desktop only, 'Q' or 'Escape' will quit.
